Who We Are

AWO360 is a cobbler and leather works company that in the production of bespoke high-quality shoes, leather belts, leather slippers, and the repair of watches. With a rich history of and excellence, providing top-notch products and exceptional repair services. Our skilled artisans and use the finest materials and traditional to create durable and stylish and leather works. We offer training for individuals who are interested in learning the art of shoe making and watch repair.

Our mission

Our mission is to uphold the tradition of quality craftsmanship and excellence in the production of footwear and the repair of timepieces. We are committed to providing our customers with superior products and services while passing on our knowledge and expertise to the next generation of craftsmen through our vocational training program.

our vision

Our vision is to be the leading cobbler company in the industry, renowned for our exceptional products, reliable repair services, and commitment to preserving and passing on the art of shoe making and watch repair.

Our core value

Craftsmanship: We are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and skill in every pair of shoes, belt, leather slipper, and watch repair that we produce.

Customer satisfaction: We prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to meet and exceed the expectations of our clients in every aspect of our service.
